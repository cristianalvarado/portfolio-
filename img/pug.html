Pug

The Pug was introduced to the United States after the Civil War,originated from China.Pugs are happy and affectionate,loyal and charming.They are not very hard to train .

Interesting facts about the pug

Size of a Male and Female Pugs are square, not more than 20 pounds. they are known as watch dogs. They are generally healthy, but are also prone to other health conditions.
